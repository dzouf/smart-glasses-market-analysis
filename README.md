# Smart Glasses Market Analysis

An analysis and forecasting project on the AI smart glasses market , 
built by aggregating public market research into interactive visualizations.

## Overview

This project explores the explosive growth of the smart glasses market 
(2024–2030) with a focus on Meta's dominant position through Ray-Ban Meta 
and the resulting impact on EssilorLuxottica's financials.

## What's inside

- **Market size forecast (2024–2030)**: year-over-year (yoy) and cumulative growth 
  projections based on Grand View Research figures
- **2025 key performance metrics**: global shipment growth , Meta's market 
  share , AI glasses share of total shipments
- **EssilorLuxottica financial impact**: revenue growth directly attributable 
  to Ray-Ban Meta sales across H1 2025 , Q3 2025 and the October 2025 stock event
- **Interactive dashboards**: built in Plotly , including a market forecast 
  chart , a market share pie chart and a revenue growth bar chart

## Data Sources

Counterpoint Research , IDC , EssilorLuxottica H1/9-month earnings reports , 
Omdia , Bank of America analyst estimates

## Tech Stack

Python , Pandas , Plotly Express , Plotly Graph Objects

## Key Insight

Meta holds a 73% share of the smart glasses market as of H1 2025 with 
Ray-Ban Meta sales tripling and directly boosting EssilorLuxottica's 
reported revenue growth , a rare case of a hardware partner's stock 
directly benefiting from an AI product's success.
